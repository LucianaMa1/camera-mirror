## 中文版

### GitHub / 小红书 / LinkedIn 通用长版

我做了一个自己真的会每天用的 macOS 小工具：`Camera Mirror`。

起因很简单。  
我经常录 demo、录 walkthrough、录教程视频。每次录屏的时候，我都希望能顺便看到自己的脸，这样我就知道自己是不是还在镜头里、表情是不是自然、位置有没有偏掉。

市面上当然有类似的 facecam / webcam mirror app。  
但很多都要每个月付十几刀。  
对我来说，这就是一个很真实的痛点：

- 我明明只是想在录屏时有一个小小的镜子
- 我不想为了这么基础的功能长期订阅
- 我更想要一个打开就能用、对不懂科技的人也友好的版本

所以我自己做了一个。

`Camera Mirror` 是一个录屏小助手。  
它不是录屏软件本身，而是一个始终置顶的小镜子窗口。

你在录视频之前打开它，就可以：

- 实时看到自己的脸
- 一边录屏幕，一边确认自己还在画面里
- 给视频加自己的 watermark 文字
- 配合任何免费的录屏软件，或者直接用苹果自带录屏

特别适合：

- 录产品 demo
- 录课程
- 录讲解视频
- 录远程演示
- 任何需要一边录屏一边注意自己镜头状态的人

如果你也有同样的痛点，欢迎试试。

## English Version

### Long Version For GitHub / LinkedIn

I built a small macOS tool that solves a very specific problem I kept running into while recording videos: `Camera Mirror`.

The reason is simple.  
I often record product demos, walkthroughs, and tutorial videos. When I record my screen, I also want to keep an eye on my face in real time, so I know whether I am still in frame, whether my expression looks natural, and whether I have drifted out of position.

There are already facecam / webcam mirror apps for this.  
But many of them charge a monthly subscription.

That was the pain point for me:

- I only needed a small on-screen camera mirror while recording
- I did not want to keep paying $10+ every month for such a basic utility
- I wanted something lightweight, simple, and friendly even for non-technical users

So I made my own.

`Camera Mirror` is a screen-recording companion for macOS.  
It is not a screen recorder itself. Instead, it gives you a small always-on-top mirror window so you can see yourself while recording.

You can use it together with:

- macOS built-in screen recording
- QuickTime Player
- any free screen recorder
- your usual video recording workflow

It is especially useful for:

- product demos
- online lessons
- tutorial videos
- remote presentations
- anyone who wants to record their screen and still keep track of their camera presence

I built it because I had the problem myself, and I did not want to keep paying a subscription for it.

### Short English Version

I built a tiny macOS screen-recording helper called `Camera Mirror`.

The whole reason was a very real pain point: I wanted to see my face while recording my screen, but I did not want to pay a monthly subscription for another facecam app.

So I made a small always-on-top mirror window for myself.  
You open it before recording, keep it on screen, and use it together with macOS screen recording, QuickTime, or any free recording tool.

Simple, lightweight, and easy enough that even non-technical users can just open it and use it.
